Key Formulas
Cost/Litre = (Seed Cost + Labour + Energy + Packaging + Overhead) ÷ QC-Passed Litres
Yield % = (Oil Out L ÷ Seeds In kg) × 100
Break-Even (L) = Fixed Costs ÷ (Avg Price − Variable Cost/L)
Gross Margin % = ((Price − Cost/L) ÷ Price) × 100
Days of Seed Stock = Seed Stock (kg) ÷ Daily Consumption (kg)
Labour/L = (Staff hrs × rate) ÷ Litres Produced
